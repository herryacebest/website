GIS Engineer

Responsibilities:

  • Design, develop, and implement geospatial solutions and enterprise GIS systems.
  • Work closely with the development team to integrate GIS applications into cloud platforms.
  • Monitor and manage GIS data pipelines and systems performance.
  • Provide advanced mapping and spatial analysis support for various teams.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Geography, Computer Science, or a related field.
  • 5+ years of experience with GIS technologies (e.g., ArcGIS, QGIS).
  • Experience in deploying GIS applications on cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure).
  • Strong programming skills (Python, Java, SQL).

Benefits:

  • Health insurance (medical, dental, vision).
  • 401(k) retirement plan with company match.
  • Paid time off (20+ days of vacation, sick leave).
  • Remote work opportunities and flexible hours.

Cloud Engineer

  • Collaborate with development teams to ensure secure and scalable cloud environments.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field.
  • 3+ years of experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Strong expertise in cloud services such as Kubernetes, Docker, and Terraform.
  • Experience in SysOps and CI/CD pipeline implementation.

Benefits:

  • Comprehensive health insurance package.
  • Retirement plan with generous employer matching.
  • Paid time off (vacation, sick leave, holidays).
  • Professional development and certification reimbursement.
